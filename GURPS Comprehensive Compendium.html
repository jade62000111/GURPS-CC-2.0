Artist†

IQ/Hard Skill

Defaults: Artist (other)−var.; IQ−6.

Prerequisites: None.

Techniques:

Specialties: Body Art; Calligraphy; Drawing; Featherworking; Hollographic/TL; Illumination; Illusion; Interior Decorating; Needlecraft; Painting; Pottery; Sculpting; Sensie Interface/TL; Woodworking.

Optional Specialties: An artist of any kind can take a further optional specialty (p. B:169) in a particular medium or technique.

Modifiers: Equipment (B:345), Time Spent (B:346).

References: B:179; Dr. Kromm [http://forums.sjgames.com/showthread.php?p=569153&highlight=Artist#post569153], [SJG Forum Profile [https://forums.sjgames.com/member.php?u=5]]; GURPS Fandom [https://gurps.fandom.com/wiki/Artist].

Two-Handed Sword Art

DX/Average Skill

Defaults: Two-Handed Sword−3; Two-Handed Sword Sport−3; Broadsword−4; Force Sword−4.

Prerequisites: None.

Techniques:

Any balanced, two-handed blade over 4 feet in length: greatswords, zweihanders, etc. This skill also covers quarterstaffs wielded like swords, as well as bastard swords, katanas, and longswords used two-handed.

Modifiers: Equipment and Time Spent.

See: Two-Handed Sword.
